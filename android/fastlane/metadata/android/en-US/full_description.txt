Welcome to Langame!

The Internet and computers have brought connectivity, but have not improved conversations. Artificial intelligence is capable of understanding us better than we understand ourselves.

Conversations are what increase transparency, cooperation and therefore prosperity, knowledge and happiness.

Langame is seeking to augment conversations in several directions:

- Help people have deeper conversations
- Help people to grow and compound their relationships
- Help people converse, regardless of their native language
- Help people expanding their knowledge through conversations
